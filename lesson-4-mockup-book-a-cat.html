How to care for a Cat

  • What they Eat? Well cat's love to eat bananas. If you're not paying attention, they'll try ot eat your face. Just pay attention to the cat. They'll take off and drive your car to the local butcher and try to buy ham with your credit card. Feed the cat so they don't try to steal your car.


    Find out more >>

  • How to talk to a Cat? Cat's love polite conversation. They know when you're talking bad about them behind their back, so don't say anything rude. They don't really know their own name, so anytime you're rude about anything, they'll think you're talking smack about them. Don't do that or they'll eat your face.


    Find out more >>

  • Teach a Cat to Drive? Good idea. They might steal your car, so best teach them how to drive to ensure they don't crash, (see our cat rental liability). Cat's can't learn how to drive stick, however, so you'll need an automatic. Cat's don't like EVs. We're not sure why. We assume it's because they don't 'purr' like a turbo-charged V6.
